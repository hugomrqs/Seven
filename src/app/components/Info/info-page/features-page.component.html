Features page

1. Search for a Movie

You can type in the search bar to look for your movie.

2. View Details of a Movie

You can check detailed information (release date, description, rating) of a specific movie by clicking on it.

3. Get Movie Suggestions

Based on the movies you explore, we suggest similar films. You can find them in the Suggestions section.

4. Rate a Movie

You can rate a selected movie. All your ratings will be available in the Ratings section.

5. List Your Favorite Movies

You can add a selected movie to your favorites. You will find all your favorite films in the Favorites section.
